The two-stage legal pressure
September 1, 2026

All large & mid-sized companies must issue and receive e-invoices. Every VAT-registered entity must be able to receive e-invoices — including micro-businesses.

September 1, 2027

VSBs, SMEs, sole proprietors: mandatory e-invoicing + e-reporting. Penalty: €250/missing submission, capped at €45,000/year.
